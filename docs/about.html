Our Story

What Is EORMC

Founded in November 2020, EORMC is a global crypto trading infrastructure provider with AI as the driver and compliance as its strategic cornerstone. Since inception, EORMC has adhered to the philosophy of "AI reshapes trading, compliance safeguards value".

The platform deeply integrates artificial intelligence technology into every aspect of crypto trading services — delivering trustworthy digital financial solutions for both individual users and institutions worldwide.

In October 2021, EORMC obtained an MSB license from FinCEN under the U.S. Department of the Treasury, successfully renewing in October 2024. The platform has since built a multi-jurisdiction compliant operating framework across 100+ countries.

Brand Identity

The EORMC Symbol

The EORMC logo is built around the letter "E", integrating the geometric dynamism of a spacecraft with the structural language of blockchain.

The top vertex represents liftoff and growth — symbolizing innovation acceleration powered by AI compute. The horizontal middle line is the central rail of the matching system, supporting high-efficiency, low-latency execution.

The lower arc represents the asset protection framework. The pointed edges extend as open interfaces — implying interoperability with multi-chain ecosystems and traditional finance.
