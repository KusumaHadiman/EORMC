Milestones

EORMC History (2020–2025)

Five years of consistent growth, innovation, and expanding global compliance.

2020
November 2020
EORMC officially founded. Core team assembled. Development strategy of "AI-driven, compliance first" established.
December 2020
Completed first institutional financing round — securing investments from multiple Silicon Valley and crypto venture funds. Total seed funding: USD $6 million.
2021
March 2021
Launched first version of the AI-powered matching trading system with spot trading. First-month trading volume exceeded USD $100 million.
October 2021
Obtained MSB license from FinCEN under the U.S. Department of the Treasury. Became one of the first AI-driven compliant trading platforms globally.
December 2021
User base surpassed 1 million. Daily trading volume stabilized at USD $500 million.
2022
July 2022
Launched intelligent wealth management module. Assets under management exceeded USD $100 million in first month.
October 2022
Released multi-chain wallet system supporting BTC, ETH, Polygon, Solana, and other major ecosystem assets.
December 2022
Hosted inaugural "AI + Fintech Innovation Summit" — gathering 300+ global experts to discuss AI applications in the crypto sector.
2023
March 2023
Awarded "Best AI Fintech Platform" by FinTech Breakthrough.
September 2023
Daily trading volume surpassed USD $5 billion. Ranked among the top 20 trading platforms globally.
December 2023
User base exceeded 10 million, covering more than 100 countries and regions worldwide.
2024
July 2024
Launched AI-driven "Transparent Proof of Reserves" system, subject to independent third-party authoritative audit.
October 2024
Successfully completed FinCEN MSB license renewal — confirming continuous U.S. federal compliance.
December 2024
Named "Most Innovative Crypto Trading Platform" by Crypto Finance Conference.
2025
January 2025
Secured strategic support from the Trump family, significantly enhancing recognition among traditional capital investors.
March 2025
Daily trading volume exceeded USD $10 billion. User base surpassed 22 million. Ranked #16 globally among crypto exchanges.
May 2025
Launched AI Open Platform providing institutional users with intelligent investment advisory, API access, and one-stop solutions.
June 2025
Launched EORMC Global Developer Program to promote innovation at the intersection of blockchain and AI.
August 2025
Obtained SEC Regulation D compliance certification — expanding authorized scope under U.S. securities law.
Future Vision

Three-Phase Roadmap

EORMC's development is structured around three ambitious phases — each building upon the last to realize the vision of a truly open, intelligent crypto universe.

EORMC 1.0
Deepening the AI-Native Financial Ecosystem
Near-Term
  • Deep integration of AI into every user interaction and risk control touchpoint, achieving a qualitative leap in trading experience quality.
  • Comprehensive development of natural language interaction and intent-driven trading models — enabling complex strategy deployment through conversational AI assistants.
  • Continuous upgrade of the AI-powered global compliance engine for real-time regulatory policy tracking and automatic multi-jurisdiction adaptation.
  • Foundation for regional operations and local service expansion across all major global markets.
EORMC 2.0
Building an Open & Interconnected Multi-Chain Universe
Mid-Term
  • Fully open the ecosystem — breaking down ecological barriers to support nearly all mainstream and emerging public chain assets under unified infrastructure.
  • Full opening of source APIs and developer platform to attract institutions and innovative projects to the AI financial cloud.
  • Focus on RWA (Real World Asset) on-chain integration and cross-border settlement innovation, connecting crypto and traditional economic systems seamlessly.
  • Establish EORMC as a truly open, interconnected value exchange network bridging digital and traditional finance.
EORMC 3.0
Shaping a Sustainable Crypto Civilization
Long-Term
  • Transcend the traditional exchange model — driving the entire crypto ecosystem toward greater fairness, inclusiveness, and sustainability.
  • Full community governance mechanisms — granting users voting and proposal rights over ecosystem development decisions.
  • AI-powered green finance initiatives: optimize global network energy efficiency, introduce asset carbon footprint tracking tools.
  • Become a key global node for crypto education — lowering barriers through accessible simulation environments to enable inclusive digital finance worldwide.

EORMC — The Launchpad to the Crypto Universe. Five years of growth; a three-phase vision for the future of digital finance.
