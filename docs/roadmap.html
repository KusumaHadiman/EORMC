Forward-Looking Analysis

Three-Phase Roadmap

EORMC's whitepaper outlines three development phases. Drexonium presents these as stated intentions — not verified commitments.

EORMC 1.0
Deepening the AI-Native Financial Ecosystem
Near-Term
  • Deep integration of AI across every user interaction and risk control touchpoint — claimed qualitative leap in trading experience.
  • Natural language and intent-driven trading models: complex strategy deployment through conversational AI assistants.
  • Continuous upgrade of the AI-powered global compliance engine for real-time regulatory policy tracking and multi-jurisdiction adaptation.
  • Regional operations expansion and local service buildout across major global markets.
EORMC 2.0
Open & Interconnected Multi-Chain Universe
Mid-Term
  • Full ecosystem opening — support for nearly all mainstream and emerging public chain assets under unified infrastructure.
  • Open APIs and developer platform to attract institutions and innovative projects to the AI financial cloud.
  • RWA (Real World Asset) on-chain integration and cross-border settlement innovation — bridging crypto and traditional economic systems.
  • Establish EORMC as an open, interconnected value exchange network spanning digital and traditional finance.
EORMC 3.0
Shaping a Sustainable Crypto Civilization
Long-Term
  • Transcend the traditional exchange model — driving the ecosystem toward greater fairness, inclusiveness, and sustainability.
  • Full community governance mechanisms granting users voting and proposal rights over ecosystem decisions.
  • AI-powered green finance initiatives: optimizing global network energy efficiency and introducing carbon footprint tracking tools.
  • Global crypto education hub — accessible simulation environments to enable inclusive digital finance worldwide.
