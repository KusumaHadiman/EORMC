Regulatory Certifications

EORMC holds active licenses from two of the world's most respected financial regulatory bodies.

License · United States
FinCEN — MSB License
Active — Renewed October 2024
EORMC holds a Money Services Business (MSB) license issued by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) under the U.S. Department of the Treasury. Originally obtained in October 2021, EORMC successfully completed the renewal audit in October 2024, demonstrating continuous compliance with U.S. federal anti-money laundering and financial crime prevention standards.

SEC — Regulation D Certification
Active — Obtained August 2025
EORMC has obtained Regulation D qualification granted by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), enabling the platform to offer certain securities-related products to accredited investors under federal exemption.
