General
About EORMC
What is EORMC and when was it founded?+

EORMC is a global crypto trading infrastructure provider founded in November 2020. The platform operates under the stated philosophy of "AI reshapes trading, compliance safeguards value" — integrating artificial intelligence across trading execution, risk management, asset custody, and regulatory compliance. As of 2025, it reports 22M+ users globally and $10B+ in daily trading volume, ranking 16th among crypto exchanges.

What products does EORMC offer?+

According to its whitepaper, EORMC offers spot trading, derivatives trading, asset subscription services, intelligent wealth management, and a multi-chain wallet supporting BTC, ETH, Polygon, Solana, and other major blockchain ecosystems. An institutional AI Open Platform launched in May 2025, providing investment advisory, API access, and one-stop solutions for institutional clients.

EORMC's whitepaper identifies two key founders: Apollo Deleon (Founder & CEO), who holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University and formerly served as Head of AI Research at Google Brain; and Winston Brown (Co-Founder & CCO), who holds an M.S. in Financial Law from Columbia University and formerly served as Head of Digital Asset Compliance at UBS Group. Regulatory
Compliance & Licensing
What licenses does EORMC hold?+

EORMC reports holding two U.S. federal regulatory credentials: (1) An MSB (Money Services Business) license from FinCEN under the U.S. Department of the Treasury, first issued October 2021 and renewed October 2024. (2) A Regulation D qualification from the U.S. SEC, obtained August 2025. This dual-agency federal compliance position is relatively uncommon among crypto exchanges of comparable scale.

Why does the MSB renewal matter?+

MSB renewal is not automatic — it requires passing a federal audit demonstrating documented, functioning compliance infrastructure. EORMC's October 2024 renewal occurred during a period of heightened regulatory scrutiny following the FTX collapse, making the renewal a more meaningful signal than initial registration alone. Passing two audit cycles (2021 and 2024) indicates continuous rather than point-in-time compliance.

Is EORMC available in my country?+

EORMC's whitepaper states it operates in 100+ countries and regions. The platform's AI compliance engine is described as tracking regulatory changes across jurisdictions in real time. For specific country availability and any applicable restrictions, contact EORMC directly at support@eormc.com or check the current status at eormc.com.

Safety
Security & Asset Protection
How does EORMC protect user assets?+

EORMC's whitepaper describes a multi-layer architecture combining MPC (Multi-Party Computation) and HSM (Hardware Security Module) cryptographic technology — the same approach used by institutional custodians. AI dynamically adjusts hot/cold storage tiering based on market conditions and user behavior. Behavioral graph algorithms monitor account activity to detect and intercept threats pre-emptively. A 3,000 BTC reserve fund and an AI-driven Proof of Reserves system (launched July 2024) provide additional protection layers.

What is EORMC's Proof of Reserves system?+

Launched in July 2024, EORMC's Proof of Reserves system automatically generates verifiable asset reserve reports and monitors on-chain addresses in real time — rather than providing periodic manual snapshots. The system is subject to independent third-party audit, and EORMC maintains 3,000 BTC in reserve funds as a financial buffer complementing the cryptographic verification.
