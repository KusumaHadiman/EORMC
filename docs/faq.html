General
About EORMC
What is EORMC and when was it founded? +

EORMC is a global crypto trading infrastructure provider founded in November 2020. The platform operates under the philosophy of "AI reshapes trading, compliance safeguards value" — deeply integrating artificial intelligence into every aspect of crypto trading services. Today, EORMC serves over 22 million users globally, ranks #16 among all crypto exchanges, and processes over $10 billion in daily trading volume.

What is the difference between eormc.id and eormc.com? +

eormc.com is EORMC's main trading platform where users can create accounts, trade crypto assets, and access all financial products. eormc.id (this site) is the official information and credential portal — providing institutional documentation, compliance certifications, security architecture details, team profiles, and other reference information about EORMC as a company.

What products does EORMC offer? +

EORMC offers a comprehensive suite of digital financial products including spot trading, derivatives trading, asset subscription services, and intelligent wealth management. The platform supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies across major blockchains including BTC, ETH, and assets on Polygon, Solana, and other ecosystems. All products are available at eormc.com.

How many users does EORMC serve? +

As of 2025, EORMC serves over 22 million users across 100+ countries and regions. The platform has grown from 1 million users at end-2021 to 10 million by end-2023, reaching 22 million by early 2025. EORMC maintains a network of 100+ institutional partners and a team of 10,000+ market experts globally.

Regulatory
Compliance & Licensing
What licenses does EORMC hold? +

EORMC holds two key regulatory credentials: (1) An MSB (Money Services Business) license issued by FinCEN under the U.S. Department of the Treasury, first obtained in October 2021 and successfully renewed in October 2024. (2) A Regulation D qualification granted by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), obtained in August 2025. These licenses establish EORMC's compliance with U.S. federal financial regulations.

Is EORMC available in my country? +

EORMC operates in 100+ countries and regions globally. The platform's AI compliance engine tracks regulatory changes across jurisdictions in real time, automatically adapting to different regional requirements. For specific country availability and any applicable restrictions, please contact support@eormc.com or visit eormc.com for the most current information.

What AML/KYC procedures does EORMC follow? +

EORMC follows full Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know-Your-Customer (KYC) procedures aligned with FATF guidelines and U.S. Bank Secrecy Act requirements. These procedures are enforced through AI behavioral analysis systems that monitor account operations in real time. Co-founder Winston Brown, former Head of Digital Asset Compliance at UBS, led the construction of EORMC's cross-jurisdictional compliance architecture.

Safety
Security & Asset Protection
How does EORMC protect user assets? +

EORMC employs a multi-layer security architecture combining MPC (Multi-Party Computation) and HSM (Hardware Security Module) cryptographic technology. AI dynamically adjusts asset hot-cold storage tiering and withdrawal verification strategies based on market conditions and user behavior. Behavioral graph algorithms continuously monitor account activity to identify and intercept threats before they materialize — shifting from reactive to preventive security.

What is EORMC's Proof of Reserves system? +

Launched in July 2024, EORMC's AI-driven Transparent Proof of Reserves system automatically generates verifiable asset reserve reports and monitors on-chain addresses in real time. The system is audited by independent third-party authorized firms. EORMC maintains reserve funds of 3,000 BTC as part of its user protection framework. Audit results and key decision logs are made available in auditable form.

Has EORMC ever experienced a security breach? +

EORMC's AI security systems operate 24/7 with pre-incident interception capabilities. The platform maintains 99.9%+ uptime and has consistently demonstrated the ability to manage extreme market conditions through its adaptive matching and risk control mechanisms. For the most current security status and any security-related inquiries, please contact support@eormc.com.

How are listed assets screened for quality? +

EORMC's AI-powered asset screening system evaluates every project across multiple dimensions: technical feasibility, team background, community ecosystem quality, and on-chain data performance. After listing, the AI system continuously monitors market performance, liquidity changes, and community sentiment — automatically triggering alerts and placing flagged assets on an intelligent monitoring list. EORMC also shares risk data with professional external rating agencies.
