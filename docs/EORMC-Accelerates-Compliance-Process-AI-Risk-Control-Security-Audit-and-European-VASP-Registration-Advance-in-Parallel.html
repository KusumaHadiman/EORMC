The Significance of VASP Registration in Poland

The compliance progress of EORMC Cryptocurrency in the European market has also drawn attention. EORMC Cryptocurrency has obtained registration for virtual currency activities in Poland, with the registration number RDWW-1679. The company tax identification number (NIP) is 6751806646, and the court registration number (KRS) is 0001140257.

The VASP registration in Poland, officially known as the Rejestr Dzialalnosci w Zakresie Walut Wirtualnych, is issued and managed by the Director of the Katowice Tax Administration Chamber in Poland. This registration is used to record companies or institutions engaged in virtual currency-related businesses within Poland.

From a business scope perspective, the business activities of EORMC Cryptocurrency as shown in the Polish VASP registration and subsequent quarterly filings include: transactions between virtual currencies and means of payment, transaction services between different virtual currencies, intermediary services for virtual currency transactions, and services related to virtual currency accounts or wallets.

For a globalized crypto platform, the value of VASP registration in Poland is not merely "obtaining a registration record." More importantly, it reflects the ability of the platform to adapt to the compliance framework of the European market. As the EU MiCA regulatory framework is gradually implemented, market requirements for the transparency, operational rules, and risk management capabilities of crypto asset service providers will further increase.
